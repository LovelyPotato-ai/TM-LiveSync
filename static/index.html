How to use TM LiveSync

1. Put your Trackmania skin files to the newly created skin/ folder located next to the executable.

2. Every time you save or export your texture over the old file, the 3D viewer will instantly update.

3. Use Left Mouse Button to rotate, Right Mouse Button to pan, and Scroll to zoom.
